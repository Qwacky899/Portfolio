Timberfall (2025)

Timberfall is my first fully developed and properly managed game. It's heavily inspired by old school Roblox hits like Lumber Tycoon 2, but built with everything I've learned over years of experimenting. For the first time, I sat down, properly planned systems, made flowcharts, blocked out features, and actually finished the thing! It's not huge or flashy, but it's functional, fun, and polished.

Squid Game Lobby

This was my attempt to create a Squid Game multiplayer lobby for a solo project. The focus was building a large-scale environment that still feels like part of the show, while experimenting with lighting, level composition, and atmosphere.

College Building Recreation

Blender project recreating the college I'm hoping to attend one day. Mainly focused on practicing accurate modeling, architectural detail, and lighting design. Really happy with how this one turned out as it taught me a lot about reference-based modeling.

Stylized Urban House

Saw a cool house on Pinterest and thought: "I wanna make that." This was a practice in hand-painted texturing and building a cozy, slightly stylized urban scene. Loved experimenting with lighting for the nighttime vibe.
